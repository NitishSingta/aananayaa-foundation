About Aananayaa Foundation

Together, We Can Make a Difference

Aananayaa Foundation began with humble roots, driven by a desire to make a positive change. Over the years, thanks to unwavering trust and support, we've grown beyond expectations, raising over $150 million in donations and products. Our programs have touched countless lives, and as our reach expands globally, so do our responsibilities.

We're committed to making every contribution count, focusing primarily on children's education, as they are the future. Beyond education, we are actively engaged in initiatives for child welfare, disability empowerment, elder care, environmental awareness, and women's empowerment.
